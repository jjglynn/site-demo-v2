Wellington's only firm audited to global fiduciary standards

CEFEX (Centre for Fiduciary Excellence) independently assesses financial advisers through a rigorous annual audit — against the highest global standards, not just local regulations.

Our certification requires us to place your interests above our own at all times. It cannot be self-declared. It must be earned and renewed every year.

Philip Stevenson and Lauren Teague hold the Accredited Investment Fiduciary© designation. This designation, for fiduciary excellence, is held by very few advisers in New Zealand.
