About us

Bloomsbury Associates was founded in Wellington in 2009 — built on the belief that New Zealanders deserved genuinely independent financial advice, free from product conflicts and focused entirely on clients' lives, not just their portfolios.

Our story

Independent advice,
from the start

Philip Stevenson founded Bloomsbury Associates in 2009 on his return to New Zealand after a career in financial services in London. He found that most NZ advisory practices were structured around product sales — not around client outcomes. He wanted to build something different: a firm whose only obligation was to the people it advised.

Today, Bloomsbury Associates is Wellington's only financial advisory practice to be CEFEX-certified — independently audited to global best practice standards every year. We work with a small number of clients, supported by a network of specialist knowledge across wealth management, law, accounting, and insurance.

We are proud supporters of the Gillies McIndoe Research Institute and Inaugural Patron Partner of the Aotearoa New Zealand Festival of the Arts.

Global best practice

Wellington's only CEFEX-certified advisers

CEFEX (Centre for Fiduciary Excellence) is an independent global assessment and certification organisation. It assesses professional financial advisers through a rigorous annual audit process.

Bloomsbury Associates was the first financial adviser practice in Wellington to be independently audited and certified to global best practice standards.

Our CEFEX certification means we exceed regulatory minimums, maintain global fiduciary best practice, and always place our clients' interests first.

Globally audited · Annual assessment

Exceeds regulatory minimums

CEFEX certification goes beyond what NZ regulations require. It benchmarks our practice against the highest global standards — independently, every year.

Independently audited

Unlike self-declared standards, CEFEX requires a rigorous third-party audit. We cannot simply claim it — we must demonstrate it.

Clients' interests always first

The fiduciary standard places your interests above all others, including our own. It is the highest duty of care in financial services — and it is what we are certified to uphold.
