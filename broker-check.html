What we look for

The five things most brokers
hope you never ask about

These are the areas where we most commonly find problems in broker portfolios. Every Broker Check report covers all five — with straight answers on each.

💸
What you're really paying

Total fees — management, brokerage, fund expenses — laid out clearly, and compared against what you should reasonably expect to pay.

📈
How the returns actually stack up

Your performance measured against the right benchmarks for your risk level — not cherry-picked figures or comparisons that make things look better than they are.

⚖️
Whether it's genuinely diversified

Are you actually spread across different assets and markets — or just spread across different products in the same market?

🏗️
Structural problems

Overlapping holdings, unnecessary complexity, tax inefficiencies — the kind of issues that quietly drag on performance and are easy to miss if you're not looking.

🔍
Whether your interests come first

We look for signs that portfolio decisions have been made to benefit the broker — not you. It's more common than most investors realise. As CEFEX-certified fiduciaries, we want to ensure clients' interests always come first.
