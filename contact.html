Common questions

What to expect when you reach out

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes — completely. We offer a free no-obligation initial consultation so that you can see if we are the right fit for you. There is no charge, no catch, and no sales pitch.

What if you're not the right firm for me?

No problem at all. If after meeting us you decide we're not for you, we'll do our best to put you in touch with someone better placed to help. We mean it.

How much money do I need to get started?

We work with clients at a range of wealth levels. The best way to find out is to have a conversation — we'll tell you honestly whether we're the right fit for your situation right now.

How do your fees work?

We are fully transparent about our fees and will explain all fees clearly during our initial consultation. We do not receive commissions from product providers — our only income is fees paid directly by clients.

Who will I meet with?

You will meet with one of our qualified Financial Advisers — typically Philip Stevenson or Lauren Teague, depending on your situation and availability.

Where are you located?

Level 9 of the Bayleys Building at 36 Brandon Street, Wellington — a two-minute walk from Wellington railway station. We can also arrange to meet at a location convenient to you.
