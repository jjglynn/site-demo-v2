What we do

We help individuals, families and charities achieve their financial goals with confidence. We know that wealth is about a lot more than money — we give our clients the clarity and structure to make the right decisions at the right time, so they can focus on what they care about most.

Service one

Financial planning

We can help plan your financial future, protecting your family and independence. We advise across a number of different areas of financial planning — to each of these we bring our expert and personal approach.

Achieving your goals relies on making the right financial decisions at the right time, over both the short and long term.

  • Retirement planning and income modelling
  • KiwiSaver strategy and optimisation
  • Insurance and risk management
  • Estate planning and wealth transfer
  • Tax-efficient investment structuring
  • Education and family goal planning

Service two

Wealth management

Most investment underperformance comes not from bad luck but from unnecessary costs, emotional decisions, and strategies built around products rather than people. We do things differently.

We build portfolios around decades of financial research, not market forecasts — low-cost, broadly diversified, and structured around your goals and timeline rather than ours.

  • Evidence-based, academic investment approach
  • Broadly diversified portfolio construction
  • Passive investment strategies
  • Regular portfolio reviews and rebalancing
  • Full transparency on fees and performance
  • Access to global investment markets

Our investment philosophy

We work with Consilium NZ, whose investment committee includes CFA charterholders with global investment experience. Our approach favours low-cost, broadly diversified passive strategies — supported by decades of financial research.

Service three

Philanthropic giving

Many of our clients have chosen causes they want to support. We offer a service whereby we can tailor that support — whether it be a named legacy, a scholarship, or active participation in a charity.

We partner with The Gift Trust to offer donor-advised fund accounts. As soon as clients put money into the account, they can receive the tax credit — while funds grow tax-free.

  • Donor-advised Gift Account setup via The Gift Trust
  • Immediate tax credit on contributions
  • Tax-free investment growth until distributed
  • Charity due diligence and research
  • Named legacies and scholarship structures
  • Endowment and trust management
Explore philanthropic giving

The Gift Trust partnership

The Gift Trust is a philanthropic charity that offers our clients a unique way to donate more efficiently and effectively. Bloomsbury manages the investments while The Gift Trust handles charity liaison and due diligence.

Service four

Ethical investing

We research ethical investment opportunities that involve socially responsible industries with sustainable business practices — without compromising returns.

For clients who want their portfolio to reflect their values, ethical investing is a core part of what we offer. We actively seek out opportunities that align with your principles.

  • ESG-screened investment portfolios
  • Exclusion of specified industries on request
  • Positive impact investment opportunities
  • Combination with philanthropic giving strategies
  • No undue compromise on financial returns
